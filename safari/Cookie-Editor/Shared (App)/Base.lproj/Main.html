Safari Cookie Cleaner

Targeted cookie cleanup for Safari on macOS and iOS.

Turn on Safari Cookie Cleaner in Settings > Safari > Extensions.

Then open Safari, tap the extensions button, and choose Safari Cookie Cleaner.

Turn on Safari Cookie Cleaner in Safari’s Extensions settings.

Safari Cookie Cleaner is on. You can turn it off in Safari’s Extensions settings.

Safari Cookie Cleaner is off. You can turn it on in Safari’s Extensions settings.

Scheduled cleanup runs best on macOS. On iPhone and iPad, Safari may delay background cleanups until the system wakes the extension again.
